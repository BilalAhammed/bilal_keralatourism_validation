Kasargode - The Land of Gods

Back
The 300 years old defensive mechanism which is the largest and well-preserved fort makes the Bekal Fort one of the best Kasaragod tourist place.
The Bela Church popularly known as ladies of sorrow church, has been set up in a gothic style which captivates archives and all those with a similar interest.
Parappa Wildlife Sanctuary - a natural habitat of slow turtle, the prickly porcupine, Malabar hornbill, Slender loris, and the cute wild jungle cat.
Carved with mythological tales of Puranas and a hidden cave leading to a pond, the Anathapura Lake Temple is well preserved for astonishing reactions.
While you hike up the upland, you can witness a wide array of flora and fauna, which will put you under a spell with its beguiling beauty at the Kotencheri Hills.
The Chandragiri Fort offers a perfect sunset view to its visitors. This bizarre fort is squarish in shape and is seated beautifully 150 feet above the sea level.
