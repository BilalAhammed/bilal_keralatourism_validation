Located on the banks of the Vembanad Lake, Kumarakom Bird Sanctuary is a paradise for bird lovers. It’s a perfect place to relax in the midst of nature.
Nestled at a height of 6000 feet above the sea level in the Western Ghats,Illikkal Kallu is one of the top Kottayam tourist places.
The Tomb of St. Alphonsa holds the mortal remains of the first Indian woman to be canonized by the Catholic Church as a saint, who was born as Anna Muttathupadathu.
Thangal Para is a hill in Kottayam which is an important pilgrimage spot for Muslims. There is a mausoleum of Sheikh Fariduddin.
Located in Erattupetta near Kottayam, Marmala waterfall is one of the places to visit if you’re looking for some adventure. It’s difficult to reach the waterfalls as only jeeps can traverse the narrow trodden paths.
Located in the popular Vembanad Lake, the Island of Pathiramanal is one of the most fascinating islands in Kerala. The journey complete with sights of rare birds, swaying coconut palms, and whispering waters.
