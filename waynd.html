Edakkal caves are well known for its pictorial depictions (cave art), which are thought to be of 6000 B C. To achieve the hollows, one needs to trek through the Ambukutty Mala.
One of the major tourist places to visit in Wayanad is Banasura Sagar Dam that impounds the famous Karamanathodu tributary of the east flowing river in Kerala that is Kabini River.
Another Wayanad tourist places which is a must visit attraction for you is the Jain Temple at Sulthan Bathery. A square rock section with a cutting of Mahavir Jain can be found in the inward sanctorum of the sanctuary.
Considered as the largest and most accurate archaeological museum in the state of Kerala, the Wayanad Heritage Museum provides an in-depth take on various facets of the history of not just the town but the entire state.
Major attraction and one of the scenic Wayanad tourist places is Meenmutty Waterfalls located at a distance of around 29 kilometers from Kalpetta. It is a famous waterfall in the state of Kerala. A three tiered waterfall is a surprising sight to be visited.
The Bamboo Forest is a vast greenery with a line of bamboos.The Nilgiri Biosphere Region additionally reaches out to an extensive extent of teak timberlands, bamboo trenches and overwhelms. A perfect safari area, deer, buffalo, cheetah and wild bears are among the spotted creatures here.
