Kappad, or Kappakadavu locally, is a beach near Koyilandy, in the district Kozhikode, where Portuguese explorer Vasco da Gama landed in 1498.
Kadalundi Bird Sanctuary is the perfect tourist spot for bird watchers. Over 100 species of native birds & 60 species of migratory birds are available there.
Kakkadampoyil is a sleepy village in Koodaranhi panchayat in Kozhikode. This village has sprung up as an idyllic tourist spot because of the scenic beauty and the famous waterfall of the same name.
The moment you arrive in the Srikanteshwara Temple, you will be engulfed by its tranquil atmosphere. Further, take time to explore the beautiful architecture and some quiet moments near the pond.
Beypore beach takes into a different world with its numerous fun-filled activities to keep you engaged as it offers abundant water activities like surfing, sunbathing, water skiing and swimming.
Kakkayam Dam is a famous picnic spot around Kozhikode because of its scenic beauty and plenty of choices for outdoor activities. Though the dam is small in size, it more than compensates with the small waterfalls around it.
